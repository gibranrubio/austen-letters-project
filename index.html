The True Art of Letter Writing

Jane claimed she had "attained the true art of letter writing, which is to say on paper exactly what you would say to a person by word of mouth." Her letters read breathlessly‚Äî"I've been talking to you as fast as I could the whole of this letter."

When you see two sheets? Jane had found free postage or hand delivery.
